This is an ongoing list of my favourite solo violin repertoire:

- Tchaikovsky Violin Concerto in D major
- Mozart Violin Concerto No. 5 III. Rondeau
- Dvorak Violin Concerto in A minor I. Allegro ma non troppo
- Elgar Cello Concerto in E minor I. Adagio - Moderato
- Prokofiev Violin Concerto No. 1 II. Scherzo: vivacissimo & III. Moderato - Allegro moderato
- Prokofiev Violin Concerto No. 2 I. Allegro moderato & III. Allegro, ben marcato
- Mozart Violin Concerto No. 4
- Wienawski Violin Concerto No. 1 I. Allegro moderato
- Wienawski Violin Concerto No. 2 II. Andante non troppo
- Butterfly Lovers Violin Concerto I. Adagio cantabile
- Rachmaninoff Rhapsody on a Theme by Paganini
- Saint-Saens Cello Concerto No. 1
- Dvorak Cello Concerto in B minor I. Allegro
- Sibelius Violin Concerto in D minor
- Korngold Violin Concerto in D major
- Brahms Double Concerto for Violin and Cello in A minor III. Vivace non troppo
